Silk Care Instructions

Silk is a luxurious fabric that requires special care. It's recommended to dry clean silk garments to maintain their integrity and appearance. If hand washing is necessary, use cold water and a mild detergent. Avoid wringing the fabric to prevent damage. Iron on low heat while the fabric is still damp to avoid burning or damaging the fibers. Always store silk garments in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

{% elif fabric == 'cotton' %}
Cotton Care Instructions

Cotton is a natural fiber known for its comfort and breathability. It's relatively easy to care for, but proper maintenance is essential to ensure the fabric's longevity. Cotton garments should be washed in cold or lukewarm water to avoid shrinkage. Use a mild detergent and avoid using bleach, as it can weaken the fabric. For stubborn stains, pre-treat with a stain remover before washing.

Dry cotton garments in the shade to prevent fading. You can either air dry them or use a tumble dryer on a low heat setting. Iron cotton fabric while it is still damp for the best results, using a high heat setting. If the fabric is dry, mist it lightly with water before ironing to smooth out wrinkles.

For colored cotton garments, wash them separately to prevent color bleeding. Always check care labels for any specific instructions that may apply to your cotton items, such as dry cleaning or special washing cycles.

{% elif fabric == 'cotton-silk' %}
Cotton-Silk Care Instructions

Cotton-silk fabric combines the comfort of cotton with the luxurious finish of silk. To maintain the fabric's quality, hand wash in cold water or machine wash on a delicate cycle. Use a mild detergent and avoid wringing the fabric. Dry flat or hang to dry, away from direct sunlight. Iron on a low setting while still damp to maintain its smoothness and shine. Always avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that may damage the delicate fabric.

{% elif fabric == 'chiffon' %}
Chiffon Care Instructions

Chiffon is a lightweight and delicate fabric. Always hand wash chiffon garments with cold water, using a mild detergent. If machine washing, place the fabric in a mesh bag to protect it from damage. Avoid wringing or twisting, as this can cause the fabric to lose its shape. Air dry the fabric and avoid direct heat. Iron with a low heat setting, and use a cloth between the iron and the chiffon to protect it from direct heat contact.

{% elif fabric == 'georgette' %}
Georgette Care Instructions

Georgette is a sheer and delicate fabric that needs to be handled with care. Hand wash in cold water with a mild detergent or use a gentle cycle on your washing machine. Always air dry georgette garments by hanging them or laying them flat. Iron with a low heat setting while the fabric is still damp, using a cloth between the fabric and the iron to prevent damage.
